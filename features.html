VeloLens was designed from the ground up for cyclists who depend on their eyewear every day — not just on weekends.

IoT Technology

How It Connects

01

Sensor Data

Built-in humidity, temperature and ambient light sensors collect real-time environmental data while you cycle.

02

Weather API

Sensor data is cross-referenced with real-time weather forecast data via the connected app over Wi-Fi or mobile data.

03

Smart Processing

The on-board processor analyses the combined data and determines when to activate the anti-fog coating or trigger alerts.

04

User Alert

You receive a visual LED indicator in the lens frame and/or a phone notification — keeping you informed without distraction.

Lens Technology

Smart Glass.
Clear Vision.

The lens system in VELO LENS uses three layers of advanced optical technology working together in real time. The result is a clear view in any weather condition.

01
Prescription Insert

Your personal optical prescription, fitted by any standard optician, slots into the inner frame.

02
Photochromic Outer Lens

The outer lens darkens in bright sunlight and clears in low light — automatically, without any manual adjustment.

03
Hydrophobic Anti-Fog Coating

Activated by the humidity sensor, this coating repels water droplets and prevents fogging — keeping vision clear in rain.
